Chingoo - Gift exchange platform

Chingoo is a gift-sharing application that was inspired by the shutdown of reddit gifts. It aims to create a community where it's members can successfully plan and execute private and public anonymous gift exchange events.

PROJECT DETAILS

Role: Sole UX Researcher and Designer

Methods: Surveys, Semi-structured Interviews, Competitive analysis, Empathy mapping, IA.

Duration: 6 weeks.

Tools: Figma, Miro.

Project Type: Client Project. (Responsive Website & Web Application).

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Gift-exchanges on the internet with strangers, as weird as it may sound, is not a new idea. In fact, it was the retirement of one of the major players in the game, Reddit gifts, that birthed the idea for Chingoo. When their favourite gift-exchange platform was officially shutting down, my clients had the idea to not just start their own, but to take it up a notch. They wanted to create a platform not just for strangers but also for family members, friends, companies, and basically anybody that wanted to plan a secret gift-exchange. This was the primary aim for the platform. The secondary aim, which was what inspired the name Chingoo (the Korean word for friend), was to also create a community where virtual strangers could maybe form lifelong friendships by virtue of their shared interest and participation in these events.

To kick-off this project, I conducted stakeholder interviews, user interviews, and secondary research to properly understand the problem space and the pain points of the users. Insights gotten from these formed the basis of the ideation, wireframing, and design that came after. 6 weeks later, Chingoo was headed for development.

GOALS & OBJECTIVES

Business Goals

  • To provide a secure and seamless application.
  • To provide a solution that inspires happiness in the users.
  • To create a community of friends through the events on the platform.

User Needs

  • An easier way to know which gifts to get for their partners during gift exchanges.
  • A more efficient way of anonymously dropping off these gifts after buying them.
  • Assurance that their partners were not going to runoff without completing the gift exchange.

After concluding the stakeholder and user research, I was able to put together the problem statement for the project.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

People perform gift-exchange events but struggle with the security of the various platforms, knowing the right gifts to get for their partners, and how to drop these gifts off.

STAKEHOLDER INTERVIEWS

To begin the project, I conducted stakeholder interviews to understand the business goals, and understand what they already knew about their target market. After this I conducted secondary research to have a better understanding of the problem domain. I then sent out screener surveys to find eligible participants for semi-structured interviews. I then conducted the interviews with the eligible participants.

Research Objectives.

  • To find out what type of gift exchanges they had previously participated in.
  • To find out the challenges they faced during those exchanges
  • To find out how they tried to solve these challenges

RESEARCH INSIGHTS

  • The business wanted to provide a secure and seamless application.
  • The business wanted to provide a solution that inspires happiness in the users.
  • The business wanted to create a community of friends through the events on the platform.
  • The users wanted an easier way to know which gifts to get for their partners during gift exchanges.
  • The users wanted a more efficient way of anonymously dropping off these gifts after buying them.
  • The users wanted assurance that their partners were not going to runoff without completing the gift exchange.

REMAINING USER CENTERED

The insights gathered during the research were used to develop a user persona and an empathy map. The aim of this was to transform our users from fictional characters in our heads to real life people with real life problems, that could encourage empathy.

PRODUCT ROADMAP

After creating the empathy map and user persona, I had a meeting with the project stakeholders to present them. Based on the insights gotten, we had a brainstorming session to create a product roadmap. The roadmap is blurred due to confidentiality.
